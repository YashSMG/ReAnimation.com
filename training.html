Civil Engineering

Our Civil Engineering program equips you with essential skills for designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure.

  • Structural analysis and design of buildings, bridges, and roads.
  • Project management and construction materials technology.
  • Software training in AutoCAD, STAAD Pro, and Primavera.

Architecture

Our Architecture training focuses on both aesthetics and functionality in building design and urban planning.

  • Hands-on training in designing modern and sustainable buildings.
  • 3D modeling, drafting, and architectural visualization.
  • Mastery of design tools like Revit, SketchUp, and 3D Max.

Mechanical Engineering

Our Mechanical Engineering program covers principles of engineering, manufacturing, and product design.

  • Thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and materials science.
  • Training in CNC machining, HVAC, and robotics systems.
  • Design and simulation using SolidWorks, CATIA, and ANSYS.

Programming

Our programming courses provide in-depth knowledge in software development and coding.

  • Languages covered: Python, Java, C++, and web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
  • Build web and mobile applications using frameworks like Django, React, and Node.js.
  • Database management, version control, and agile development practices.
